about us
We are an award-winning marketing firm that matches brands with high-mileage car owners who want to drive with an ad decal on their car. Since 2012, we have put thousands of wrapped cars on the road for brands throughout the entire country. Through this mutually beneficial relationship, brands use Carvertise to get their message seen in certain geographic locations while drivers join us to make money for something they already do.
